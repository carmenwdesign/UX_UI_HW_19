About me

As a UX Designer with experience in 3D modelling, graphic design, and textiles, I bring a unique perspective and a love for all things creative to every project. My approach to problem solving has always been centred on empathy. By focusing on the details and understanding the needs of the end user, we can create products that connect on an emotional level, creating impactful and meaningful designs.

I love being able to collaborate to generate innovative ideas and crafting effective outcomes. It's inspiring to see different perspectives merge and contribute to the final product. I am always eager to explore new territories and dedicated to continually seeking new ways to improve and push boundaries.
